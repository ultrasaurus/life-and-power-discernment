Trayce Peterson (She/Her) is a native of Philadelphia, PA, and received her B.A. and Master's of Divinity from Earlham College and Earlham School of Religion. She is a member general committee for Friends Committee on National Legislation and on AFSC Standing Nominating Committee Trayce is an instructor at the University of Arizona in Human Rights Practice. She co-founded SplitSeed Productions, which uses art-based interventions to inform, educate, and explore human rights and justice issues. As a community organizer, she works with several local coalitions.
